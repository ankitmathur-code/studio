# **App Name**: TuneSpotlight

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: A prominent section displaying the track title and artist name, capturing immediate attention.
- Embedded Music Video: Integration of a music video from platforms like YouTube or Vimeo for visual engagement.
- Custom Audio Player: A sleek, custom-designed audio player with play/pause functionality, volume control, and a progress bar.
- Loop Toggle: A button to enable continuous looping of the audio track for repeat listening.
- Session Play Counter: Displays the number of times the track has been played by the user within their current browsing session.
- Share Button: Allows users to easily share the track promotion page on various social media platforms or via a direct link.
- Lyrics/Notes Section: A dedicated area to display lyrics, liner notes, or additional information about the music track.

## Style Guidelines:

- Dark color scheme, inspired by music tech with a retro boom bap feel. Primary color: a rich, deep magenta (#A329A3) to convey energy and creativity, standing out against dark tones.
- Background color: A very dark, desaturated purple (#1F141F) to provide a moody and modern canvas that allows primary elements to pop.
- Accent color: A vibrant, brighter purple (#9952E0) used for interactive elements, highlights, and subtle bursts of energy, analogous to the primary color.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its computerized, bold, and tech-inspired look, suitable for track titles and artist names. Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for legibility in smaller text like lyrics and notes, maintaining a modern and objective feel.
- Clean, geometric icons for playback controls, share actions, and loop toggle, fitting the modern tech aesthetic. Avoid overly ornate or realistic icons.
- A single-page, responsive layout optimized for mobile-first experience. Content sections are clearly delineated with ample negative space to enhance readability and focus. Features a prominent hero section for immediate impact.
- Smooth, subtle animations for UI transitions, button hovers, and progress bar updates, adding to a premium and interactive user experience. Focus on fluidity without distraction.
